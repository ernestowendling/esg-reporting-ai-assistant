# Synthetic Principal Adverse Impact Statement — Reporting year 2025

## Reporting basis

This fictional statement covers the Synthetic Green Transition Fund for the year ending 31 December 2025. Values are illustrative and must not be interpreted as real investment data.

## Principal adverse impact results

PAI 1, greenhouse-gas emissions, was measured at 118,400 tonnes of carbon-dioxide equivalent. PAI 3, greenhouse-gas intensity of investee companies, was 146 tonnes of carbon-dioxide equivalent per million euros of revenue.

PAI 10, violations of the UN Global Compact principles and OECD Guidelines, affected 0.7% of portfolio value. The internal review threshold was 0.5%, so this indicator exceeded the threshold and was escalated for review.

PAI 14, exposure to controversial weapons, was 0.0% of portfolio value. The review found no exposure to anti-personnel mines, cluster munitions, chemical weapons or biological weapons.

## Remediation and control response

For the PAI 10 threshold breach, the ESG Oversight function opened control issue ESG-2025-017. The assigned action was enhanced issuer due diligence and a documented engagement plan. Closure requires evidence of review by the Sustainability Committee.
